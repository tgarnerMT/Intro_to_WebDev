Rimworld: A Hardcore Colony Survival Game

Overview:

Simply put, Rimworld is a colony management survival game designed by Tynan "Ty" Sylvester.
The game is designed to be a punishing storytelling experience about strife and struggle, with the hope of survival and the guarantee of eventual failure.
The game has multiple modes and variables which will be explained elsewhere. Needless to say, the game is diverse and full of content.

Rimworld is widely considered one of the best games created in the modern era due to its nearly infinite replayability and its diverse gameplay style.
